Your participation ensures that each OES message will be delivered to our San Diego community members in a timely and efficient manner during crisis or disaster situations.

Goals of this volunteer program:
  • Improve the Alert Warning effectiveness during crisis or disaster situations
  • Monitor social media messages and provide real-time observations from the emergency

Our collaborative efforts will result in improving our community response to emergencies and the safety of residents. Let's work together to keep San Diego safe!
